#+TITLE: Cauchy-Schwarz inequality
#+FILETAGS: :concept:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

The *Cauchy-Schwarz inequality* depends on the notion of a [[id:9c711ea1-c9de-4e90-96cf-e7710bd11aeb][vector space]].

#+NAME: Cauchy-Schwarz inequality
#+begin_theorem latex
Let \( \vert V \rangle \) and \( \vert W \rangle \) be elements of a vector space with norms \( |V| \) and \( |W| \) respectively. We then have
\begin{align*}
|\langle V \mid W\rangle| \leqslant|V||W|.
\end{align*}
#+end_theorem
