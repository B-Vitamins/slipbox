#+TITLE: Measurement result
#+FILETAGS: :concept:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

A *measurement result* is the outcome of a [[id:c86a0c88-e04a-4c94-9edb-2b61337411c0][measurement]]. A measurement result is well-defined if and only if it consists of a [[id:0e8d8303-1f24-4a88-b550-1da444245036][measurement value]] and a [[id:e578ae1b-f24b-4de2-9e0a-292909cb8cfd][measurement uncertainty]]. A measurement value without a measurement uncertainty is meaningless.
