#+TITLE: Upper bound
#+FILETAGS: :concept:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

The definition of upper bound depends on the notion of [[id:c4fd37c6-3784-44d2-84ec-a75d01bc6b17][partial order]].

#+NAME: Upper bound
#+begin_definition latex
The upper bound of a subset \(S\) of a partially ordered set \((P, \leq)\) is an element \(b\) of \(P\) such that \(b \geq x\) for all \(x \in S\).
#+end_definition
