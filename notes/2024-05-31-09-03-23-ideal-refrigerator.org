#+TITLE: Ideal refrigerator
#+FILETAGS: :literature:spop:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

An *ideal refrigerator* is like an [[id:e23a0800-61ba-4942-addd-92734a2bf6da][ideal heat engine]] running backward, that is, using work \(W\) to extract [[id:5211418e-55fa-4d7e-b2f5-88148f84cc76][heat]] \(Q_{C}\) from a cold system, and dumping heat \(Q_{H}\) at a higher [[id:73293bf4-e866-4339-ad08-4258cebfb93f][temperature]].

The *efficiency* of a refrigerator is defined as

\[
\omega=\frac{Q_{C}}{W}=\frac{Q_{C}}{Q_{H}-Q_{C}}
\]
