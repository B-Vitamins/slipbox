The definition of a *orthonormal basis* depends on the notion of a [[id:f722b69c-fda5-487b-aa69-6d6dd687eb84][orthogonal basis]].

#+NAME: Orthonormal Basis
#+begin_definition latex
An orthogonal basis whose basis vectors are normalized is said to form an orthonormal basis.
#+end_definition
