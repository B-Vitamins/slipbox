#+TITLE: Orthogonality
#+FILETAGS: :concept:linalg:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

The definition of *orthogonal vectors* depends on the notion of a [[id:bd078855-c4e2-4c48-b2ae-8a16b01a9547][inner product]].

#+NAME: Orthogonality
#+ATTR_LATEX: :environment definition
#+begin_definition
\(|V\rangle\) and \(|W\rangle\) are called orthogonal (to each other) iff \(\langle V \mid W\rangle=0\).
#+end_definition
