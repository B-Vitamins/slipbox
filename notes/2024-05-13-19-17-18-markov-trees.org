#+NAME: Markov Trees
#+begin_definition latex
In the case of a Markov network, a tree is defined as a graph in which there is one, and only one, path between any pair of nodes.
#+end_definition

A [[id:f075c4f0-69aa-4f72-8879-42180ea7a063][Markov network]] that is a tree does not have loops.
