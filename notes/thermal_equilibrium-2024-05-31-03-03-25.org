#+TITLE: Thermal equilibrium
#+FILETAGS: :literature:prml:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org
A [[id:39fcc45a-9b91-42f9-9960-19eab99aae83][thermodynamic system]] is said to be in a state of *thermal equilibrium* when the [[id:df6bf294-bc8a-441f-b16a-02fe248d59a9][state functions]] that describe it are invariant over observation timescales. Equilibrium is observation-time-dependent (e.g., glass flows over millennia but is solid over decades).
