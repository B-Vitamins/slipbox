#+TITLE: Ideal heat engine
#+FILETAGS: :literature:spop:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

An *ideal heat engine* works by taking in a certain amount of [[id:5211418e-55fa-4d7e-b2f5-88148f84cc76][heat]] \(Q_{H}\), from a heat source (for example a coal fire), converting a portion of it to work \(W\), and dumping the remaining heat \(Q_{C}\) into a heat sink (e.g., atmosphere).

The *efficiency* of the engine is calculated from

\[
\eta=\frac{W}{Q_{H}}=\frac{Q_{H}-Q_{C}}{Q_{H}} \leq 1 .
\]
