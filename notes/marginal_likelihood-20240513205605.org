#+TITLE: Marginal likelihood
#+FILETAGS: :literature:prml:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

A synonym for [[id:7a527c55-77eb-4287-b09e-5af08d301b39][model evidence]]. It is so called because the model evidence can be viewed as a likelihood function over the space of models, in which the parameters have been marginalized out.
