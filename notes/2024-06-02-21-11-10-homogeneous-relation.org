The definition of a homogeneous relation depends on the notion of [[id:8435eda4-5769-4d55-8890-2bf59018ef00][binary relation]].

#+NAME: Homogeneous relation
#+begin_definition latex
Let \(R\) be a binary relation over sets \(X\) and \(Y\). When \(X=Y\), the binary relation \( R \) is called a homogeneous relation or endorelation.
#+end_definition

See [[id:6df53438-8d45-4a80-b969-f30040318d8a][Heterogeneous relation]].
