#+TITLE: Ordered pairs
#+FILETAGS: :concept:

The definition of ordered pairs depends on the notion of [[id:c974fc24-8cb0-42c7-85ca-9ca76a77a1d6][Cartesian products]].

#+NAME: Ordered pairs
#+begin_definition latex
Given sets \(X\) and \(Y\), the elements of the Cartesian product \(X \times Y\) of sets \( X \) and \( Y \) are called ordered pairs.
#+end_definition
