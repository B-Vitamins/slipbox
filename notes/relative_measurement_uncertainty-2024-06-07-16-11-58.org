#+TITLE: Relative measurement uncertainty
#+FILETAGS: :concept:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

The *relative measurement uncertainty* is the ratio of the [[id:e578ae1b-f24b-4de2-9e0a-292909cb8cfd][measurement uncertainty]] to the absolute value of the [[id:0e8d8303-1f24-4a88-b550-1da444245036][measurement value]] (provided the measurement value is nonzero).
