#+TITLE: Linear span
#+FILETAGS: :concept:linalg:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

The definition of the *linear span* of a set of /linearly independent vectors/ depends on the notion of a [[id:9c711ea1-c9de-4e90-96cf-e7710bd11aeb][vector space]] and [[id:c44d4e2b-ff20-4fcf-b3a8-fe2ba0b50352][linear independence]].

#+NAME: Span
#+ATTR_LATEX: :environment definition
#+begin_theorem latex
Any \(|v\rangle \in V^n\) may be written as a linear combination of \(n\) linearly independent vectors \(\{11\rangle, \ldots,|n\rangle\}\).
#+end_theorem
