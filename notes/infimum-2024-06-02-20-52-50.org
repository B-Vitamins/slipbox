#+TITLE: Infimum
#+FILETAGS: :concept:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

The definition of infimum depends on the notion of [[id:49435674-c64b-4a19-8c7e-81c15a00c8d2][lower bound]].

#+NAME: Lower bound
#+begin_definition latex
Let \(S\) be a subset of a partially ordered set \((P, \leq)\). A lower bound \(a\) of \(S\) is called an infimum or greatest lower bound or meet of \(S\) if for all lower bounds \(y\) of \(S\) in \(P, y \leq a\), i.e., \(a\) is larger than any other lower bound.
#+end_definition
