#+TITLE: Extensivity
#+FILETAGS: :literature:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org


*Extensivity* refers to a property of [[id:df6bf294-bc8a-441f-b16a-02fe248d59a9][state functions]], say \( f(X_1, X_2, \ldots, X_n) \), for which the following constraint holds

\[
f(\lambda X_1, \lambda X_2, \ldots, \lambda X_n)= \lambda f(X_1, X_2, \ldots, X_n),
\]
