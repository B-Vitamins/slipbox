#+TITLE: Heterogeneous relation
#+FILETAGS: :concept:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

The definition of heterogeneous relation depends on the notion of [[id:8435eda4-5769-4d55-8890-2bf59018ef00][binary relation]].

#+NAME: Homogeneous relation
#+begin_definition latex
Let \(R\) be a binary relation over sets \(X\) and \(Y\). When \(X \neq Y\), the binary relation \( R \) is called a heterogeneous relation.
#+end_definition

See [[id:55412a61-9999-4282-871f-744368c7d5ae][Homogeneous relation]].
