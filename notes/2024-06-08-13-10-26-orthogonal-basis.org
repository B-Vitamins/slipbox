#+TITLE: Orthogonal basis
#+FILETAGS: :concept:linalg:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

The definition of a *orthogonal basis* depends on the notion of [[id:6764a03f-7428-4357-a45c-57d203fd69f8][orthogonality]] and [[id:f8d62dcb-134e-4a07-bbde-6e1672c3d6fb][basis]].

#+NAME: Orthonormal Basis
#+begin_definition latex
A set of basis vectors, that are pair-wise orthogonal are said to form an orthonormal basis.
#+end_definition
