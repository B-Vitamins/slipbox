#+TITLE: Fixed-precision arithmetic
#+FILETAGS: :concept:
#+SETUPFILE: ~/.config/emacs/setup/setupfile.org

In *fixed-precision arithmetic*, calculations are performed on numbers whose digits of precision are limited by the /word length/ of the particular processor design.

+ Fixed-precision arithmetic is done using hardware arithmetic (ALU and FPU).
